Features

NobelGT is a better alternative for the course search features provided in Oscar (get the pun?). It is also a strong alternative to Courseoff that takes the burden of choosing a good schedule off of your shoulders, and gets you the best possible schedule for your needs.
grade

Easy To Use

You type in your classes and pick your criteria, and NobelGT does the rest for you. You can navigate possible schedules based on similarity to each other and the score they have received according to your criteria.

schedule

Fast

You receive results as they are calculated by our engine. You get a list of CRN codes for all schedules so that you can easily register for classes once you found your perfect fit.

lightbulb_outline

Smart

NobelGT uses deep learning techniques and human-rated schedules for most criteria, so that the results you receive are as good as can be - removing errors that can stem from arbitrary weighing of your criteria.
